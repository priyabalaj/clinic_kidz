Our Pediatric Services

Dr. ST Pushpa provides comprehensive pediatric care with a focus on preventive medicine, early intervention, and family-centered care. Our services are designed to support your child's healthy development from infancy through adolescence.

Vaccination Services

Vaccination Schedule (as per IAP)

Comprehensive immunization services following the Indian Academy of Pediatrics (IAP) guidelines. We ensure your child receives all recommended vaccines at the right time to provide optimal protection against preventable diseases.

Vaccine Safety & Monitoring

Safe vaccination practices with proper monitoring and follow-up care to ensure optimal protection and minimal side effects for your child's health and well-being.

Vaccination Records & Tracking

Detailed vaccination records and tracking system to ensure your child stays up-to-date with all required immunizations and health protection.

Growth & Development

Growth Monitoring

Regular monitoring of height, weight, and head circumference to ensure your child is growing at a healthy rate and reaching important developmental milestones.

Developmental Milestones

Tracking important developmental milestones to ensure your child is progressing normally in all areas of development including motor, cognitive, and social skills.

Growth Charts & Analysis

Detailed growth charts and analysis to identify any potential growth issues early and provide appropriate interventions for optimal development.

Child Care Services

Nutrition Guidance

Expert advice on proper nutrition for different age groups, from breastfeeding support to healthy eating habits for your child's optimal growth and development.

Common Illness Treatment

Treatment and management of common childhood illnesses like fever, cold, cough, and infections with gentle, effective care to help your child recover quickly.

Emergency Care

24/7 emergency pediatric care for urgent situations. We're here when you need us most, providing immediate medical attention for your child's health emergencies.
