Our Pediatric Services

Dr. ST Pushpa provides comprehensive pediatric care with a focus on preventive medicine, early intervention, and family-centered care. Our services are designed to support your child's healthy development from infancy through adolescence.

Newborn & Infant Care

Specialized care for newborns and infants, including routine check-ups, feeding guidance, and developmental monitoring.

  • Newborn health assessment
  • Breastfeeding support
  • Growth & development tracking
  • Vaccination schedule
  • Sleep pattern guidance

Vaccination Services

Complete immunization services following IAP guidelines to protect your child from preventable diseases.

  • Travel vaccines

  • Post-vaccination monitoring
