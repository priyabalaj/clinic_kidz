About Dr. ST Pushpa

Education & Qualifications

Experience

With over 12 years of dedicated service in paediatric care, Dr. ST Pushpa has established herself as one of Bangalore's most trusted paediatricians. Her journey includes:

  • Specialist in Neonatal Intensive Care

Dr. Pushpa believes in a holistic approach to child healthcare, combining evidence-based medicine with compassionate care. Her practice focuses on:

  • Preventive healthcare and wellness
  • Early detection and intervention
  • Family-centered care approach
  • Continuous education and support for parents
