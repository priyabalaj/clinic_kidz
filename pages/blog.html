Changing Weather Makes Many Children Vulnerable to Infections

August 28, 2024 • Health Alert • Featured Article

Bengaluru doctors report a significant spike in viral infections among children, directly linked to the soaring humidity and erratic temperature changes in the city. The unusually high humidity levels are creating an ideal breeding ground for viruses, bacteria, and fungi, leading to a surge in respiratory and gastrointestinal infections.

Dr. Sampath Kumar Shettigar, senior consultant in pediatrics and neonatology, emphasizes that adverse weather changes are significantly impacting children's health, causing respiratory issues, allergies, and viral infections. The constant adjustments the body has to make to varying temperatures compromise natural defense mechanisms, making children more susceptible to infections.
